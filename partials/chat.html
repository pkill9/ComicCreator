What's ComicShare?

  • ComicShare is an application for creating and sharing short comic stories with you friends!

  • Invite your friends to create a comic with you based on anything you like!

  • Utilize creative tools to flex your artistic side and watch as a comic grows throughout the correspondencse

  • Work on your story and close it off whenever you like - and then start again!
